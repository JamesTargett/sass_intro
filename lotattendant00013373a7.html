Lot Attendant

Pacific Honda.

North Vancouver Honda Dealer has an opening for an Automotive Lot Attendant.

We have an opening for a progressive and motivated individual to join our Service Team. Individual requires a very strong work ethic with a professional and courteous.

Major Responsibilities:

  • Match serial numbers of incoming vehicles against invoices, as necessary.
  • Register and store keys.
  • Wash and clean customer vehicles, as required.
  • Ensure customer cars are readily available for pick-up when required.
  • Perform nightly check to ensure that all customer vehicles are locked and windows closed.
  • Walk customers to vehicles if appropriate upon completion of service work.
  • Drive customers to their destinations in lieu of dealership transportation, as needed.
  • Follow all company and manufacturer policies, directives and procedures.
  • Comply with required health and safety regulations.
  • Perform any other duties as required.

Qualifications

  • High School Diploma
  • Valid BC Drivers License
  • Able to drive both Manual and automatic.
  • Basic understanding of automotive terminology

Compensation

  • Full Training & Support
  • Wonderful Working Environment
  • Medical and Dental benefit plan

Benefits
We offer full benefits including dental insurance, medical insurance, prescription drug coverage, life insurance, and a vision-care plan.

About the Company
Pacific Honda is a family owned and operated dealership that has been in business since 1973. It is located in the North Shore Automall in one of B.C.'s largest Honda showroom and service facilities. Pacific Honda sells, leases and finances new and used automobiles. It has a 21 bay service outlet and supports a retail and wholesale parts department. Pacific Honda stands out beyond others due to its extensive and very successful product line which is continually touted as the benchmark amongst competitors. Its business is supported by professional and customer friendly staff who are all too pleased to make clients buying and servicing experiences pleasant and rewarding. Pacific Honda has serviced the North Shore for over 35 years and is now housed in one of Vancouver's newest and most modern Honda dealership facilities.

About the Community
Pacific Honda is located in beautiful North Vancouver known for having a growing middle class, exclusive neighborhoods, a diverse population, top-notch public schools, clean air, safe, clean water, convenient public transportation, great oceanside views, mountainous regions, a woodsy environment, a spectacular cityscape, and a breathtaking landscape.
